Workflow families

Three hard-coded university flows

Student fee collection, university treasury distribution, and scholarship/refund routing all sit inside the same KeeperKit-backed operating model.

  • Student prompts sweep assets from wallets, DeFi, and exchanges.
  • Staff workflows split incoming money into campus operating channels.
  • Exception workflows handle refunds, bursary payouts, and emergency support.
